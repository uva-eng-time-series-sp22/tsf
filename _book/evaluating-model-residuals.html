22.1 Model residuals vs. forecast errors

Model residuals:

Your data: \(y_1, y_2, \ldots, y_T\)

Fitted values: \(\hat{y}_1, \hat{y}_2, \ldots, \hat{y}_T\)

Model residuals: \(e_t = y_t - \hat{y}_t\)

Forecast errors:
